Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

CareBridge Health Marketing ("CareBridge," "we," "us," or "our") provides marketing services to healthcare providers in South Florida. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. This policy applies to carebridgehealth.marketing and does not apply to the websites, forms, or systems of the healthcare practices we work with.

This site does not collect patient health information. This website is for business inquiries from healthcare providers and organizations only. Please do not submit any patient names, health conditions, diagnoses, treatment information, or other protected health information (PHI) through this site.

Information we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the information you choose to provide, which may include:

  • Your name and the name of your practice or organization
  • Your email address
  • Your specialty or category of practice
  • Any message or details you choose to include about how we might help

We do not use cookies, advertising pixels, or cross-site tracking technologies on this website as of the effective date above. If that changes — for example, if we add privacy-conscious analytics — we will update this policy and, where required, request your consent.

How we use your information

We use the information submitted through our contact form only to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule a consultation or audit
  • Understand your practice's needs so we can tailor our recommendations
  • Maintain internal records of business inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

How your information is stored

Contact form submissions are stored using Supabase, a secure third-party database provider, and this website is hosted on Vercel. Both providers maintain their own security and privacy practices for the infrastructure they operate. Access to submitted information is limited to CareBridge personnel who need it to respond to your inquiry.

Your choices and rights

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the information you've submitted at any time by emailing hello@carebridgehealth.marketing. We will respond within a reasonable time.

Children's privacy

This website is intended for healthcare business professionals and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13 years of age.

Third-party links

Our footer links to external resources, including guidance published by the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) and the Federal Trade Commission (FTC). Those websites have their own privacy practices, and we encourage you to review them independently.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@carebridgehealth.marketing.
