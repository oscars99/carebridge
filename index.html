Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Straight answers about bilingual campaigns, HIPAA-aware practices, timelines, and how we work with South Florida providers.

Do you only work with the specialties listed on this page?

Our core focus is dermatology, dental, primary/urgent care, behavioral health, and similar high-consideration specialties across South Florida — but we regularly support other healthcare and wellness providers with the same bilingual, compliance-aware approach. Reach out and we'll tell you honestly whether we're a fit.

How do you keep patient information out of your marketing and AI tools?

We never accept protected health information (PHI) through this site or in campaign work unless a signed business associate agreement and documented policies are in place. Every AI-assisted draft goes through clinician or owner review before anything is published, and our compliance section above outlines the full list of guardrails we follow.

Are your Spanish campaigns translated from English, or written natively?

Written natively. We build separate messaging, personas, and ad angles for Spanish-speaking audiences instead of running your English ads through a translator — that's the difference between reaching South Florida's bilingual market and just talking past it.

How long until we see results?

Most practices see the audit and roadmap within the first two weeks, and initial campaign or content results (leads, calls, ranking movement) within 30–60 days depending on the service line and how competitive your market is. Multi-location and SEO-heavy work typically builds over 3–6 months.

Do we have to sign a long-term contract?

No. The Starter package is a one-time engagement. Growth and Premium are month-to-month partnerships — we'd rather earn the renewal every month than lock you into a term that doesn't fit your practice.

Is the HIPAA-Safer Funnel Audit really free?

Yes. You'll get a prioritized list of privacy and compliance risks in your current website, forms, and tracking setup, plus a 30-day roadmap, with no obligation to continue working with us afterward.

Can you work with our existing website, EHR, or CRM?

In most cases, yes. We audit what's already in place — your site, forms, chat tools, and lead routing — before recommending changes, so we're not asking you to rip out systems your front desk already knows how to use.

Do you support practices with multiple locations?

Yes — our Premium package is built specifically for multi-location groups that need consistent campaigns, shared reporting, and coordinated budget across every office.
